Frequently asked

What people usually ask before booking

Where do I start?

With a consultation — in person or online, 60 minutes, €70. During it I will define your individual constitution, find problem areas, and tell you whether you need Panchakarma, separate treatments, or simply a change in nutrition.

How do I prepare for the first consultation?

It helps to have recent blood test results (general and biochemistry) and notes on any chronic conditions you have. Think about what bothers you most and for how long. You do not need to remove anything specific from your diet. If the consultation is online — prepare a quiet space and a stable internet connection.

Can Ayurveda replace conventional medicine?

No, and I never suggest that. Ayurveda complements modern medicine: it works where pills only mask the symptom, and it helps the body cope on its own. Acute conditions always need conventional medical care.

How long does a Panchakarma course last and what is included?

From 7 to 21 days. Any course includes: an individual plan after the consultation, daily oil treatments, herbs and oils, an Ayurvedic diet, and the doctor's daily care. The price depends on the length and content of the program — we discuss it after the consultation.

When is the next appointment day in Moscow?

I visit Moscow on appointment days — usually once every 1–2 months. Current dates and the address I send on request by WhatsApp or Telegram. If something is urgent — an online consultation can be arranged within the same week.

Are there any contraindications?

Yes. Acute conditions, decompensated chronic illness, and pregnancy. All of this is discussed during the first consultation — if needed I will recommend extra tests.

How is an online consultation different from an in-person one?

In person I can see more (pulse, skin, posture). Online is excellent diagnostics through symptoms, history, nutrition, and lifestyle. For patients in other cities and countries the online format works very well.

How do I pay?

For in-person treatments — on site, by cash or bank transfer. For online consultations and course bookings — by agreement through messengers: Russian or international transfers, choose what suits you.
